Mr.Vinay Singh

Education is a life-long learning process. Learning happens all the time; not only in a pre-designated place called the school. It happens in the home, between home and school too. The home’s and school’s mission therefore is to provide a learning environment and opportunities to the children as the learners. Shaping young, impressionable minds is one of life’s biggest challenges. At Vinay Vidhya Niketan, Patna , we aim to empower our students to grow as individuals with strong open discerning minds with an international perspective, preparing them to make a mark in the global village - the world has come to be today. We value igniting the spark of creativity and curiosity in our students.
